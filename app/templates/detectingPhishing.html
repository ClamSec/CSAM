Social Engineering: Fake Websites [5/6]

Phishing emails will often take you to fake websites that are mocked up to look like legitimate websites. For example, compare:
CougarNet Login
and
CougarNet Login
It should be obvious that the second link is the legitimate login page. In a real attack scenario, the fake webpage would look almost exactly like real one. CHECK THE URL. A fake website will rarely have the correct URL. It will instead have a URL like "columbusstate.college.com" or "columbusstate.co" instead of the "real" URL.
